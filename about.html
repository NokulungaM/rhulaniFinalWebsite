Rhulani Mokwena Foundation



The Rhulani Mokwena Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming lives through community engagement programs, football coaching master class programs, sporting development initiatives, and educational involvement programs. Our vision is to create a positive impact in underprivileged communities by providing access to quality education, sports training, and community development opportunities. We firmly believe that those with the power to help have the responsibility to help, and our values reflect a commitment to high moral standards and social responsibility.

Mission Statement: To empower individuals from underprivileged backgrounds through educational resources, sports coaching, and community development programs, fostering positive social change and creating opportunities for a brighter future.
